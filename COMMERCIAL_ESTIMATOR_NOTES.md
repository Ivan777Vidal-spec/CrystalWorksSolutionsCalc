# Commercial Estimator Notes

The estimator now uses task-based starter production standards rather than residential packages or flat pricing formulas.

## Important

The task minutes are initial planning assumptions. Crystal Works Solutions should replace them with production data gathered from real site walkthroughs and completed contracts.

## Current estimator flow

1. Define facility and service schedule.
2. Add room and area types.
3. Select included tasks for every area.
4. Calculate recurring labor hours.
5. Recommend crew size from the target shift length.
6. Calculate wages, payroll burden, supervision, travel, materials, overhead, contingency, revenue, and profit.

Cubicles are estimated by workstation count. Desktop wiping is kept separate because customers may not permit cleaners to handle papers or personal property.
